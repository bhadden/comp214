Install and Test a Freeware Voice Command/Dictation Program

Siri

Siri is a virtual assistant program, developed for an number of Apple Inc. devices, such as iPhone, iPad, macOS and watchOS. Siri was originally a spinoff company of SRI International and was released as a standalone iPhone app in 2010. Apple acquired the company only two months later and Siri was integrated into the iPhone 4S for its launch.

The program uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to interpret and act on voice queries. It is comprised of three components:

Conversational Interface

This interface emulates conversation with a human. Instead of text or graphics-based interfaces that output results to just a screen, the conversational user interface allows the computer to communicate with the user in their natural language.

Personal context awareness

Allows Siri to understand the context of the request being made. Siri can gradually adapt to the users preferences and personalize results over time. If you’re listening to a song in the background and ask “what album is this from?”, Siri knows you mean the current song being played.

Service delegation

Allows Siri to combine data from various services providers and data sources in order to optimally handle and answer a user request. If a user requests information about a restaurant, Siri will gather information from many sources, such as local business directories, maps, guides and reviews in order to show results that meet the user’s request.
