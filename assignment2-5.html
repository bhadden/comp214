Assignment 2.5

Describe a Large-Scale Display System

Mercedes-Benz Stadium Halo Board

The Mercedes-Benz Stadium, home to the Atlanta Falcons NFL team, houses one of the largest single display boards in the world. Aptly-named the “Halo”, it is a 58’ x 1100’ ring-shaped display board that sits just below the roof of the stadium and occupies 62,350 worth of real-estate. It was custom-built by the company Daktronics, which makes many large-scale displays for stadiums and arenas around the world. If you were to stretch out the display into a single column, it would be taller than the Eiffel tower.

This type of low-fidelity prototyping can be quick and simple, but highly effective at allowing creators and developers easily and efficiently bring early ideas to life. This can be useful in providing proof-of-concept to interested parties and can help convey the big-picture or end-goal early on in the development cycle. It not only provides an early chance to visualize, but also helps with solving UI logic and navigation elements.
